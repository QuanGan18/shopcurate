I'VE RECEIVED A FAULTY ITEM, WHAT SHOULD I DO?

Last updated 11/22/2019 6:11 PM

We want to sort out any issues with faulty items straight away.

If you discover a fault, please contact our Customer Care team using the options at the bottom of this page with the order number, the faulty item's name and number, and a description of the fault.


DID WE RESOLVE YOUR ISSUE?
