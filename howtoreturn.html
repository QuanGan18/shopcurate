HOW DO I RETURN ITEM(S) TO CURATE?

Last updated 11/22/2019 6:11 PM

Returns option
Return through UPS Ground use one of the 47,000 locations nationwide and use the peel off label included in your parcel.
Do I need proof of postage?
As the parcel remains your responsibility until it arrives with us, remember to keep hold of your proof of postage so if your parcel goes missing you'll have proof you sent it.
