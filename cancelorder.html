CAN I CANCEL MY ORDER?

Last updated 11/22/2019 6:11 PM

If you need to cancel your order for any reason there are varying time limits in which you can cancel your order depending on what shipping option you've chosen:

Express Shipping - 15 minutes after completing your order
Standard Shipping - 60 minutes after completing your order
