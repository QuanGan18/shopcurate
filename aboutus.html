WHERE BUSINESS MEETS FASHION.

CURATE founders Matthew Lee and Donovan Cherng were both raised in urban Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia where they both had huge interest in men's fashion since a very young age. While studying abroad in San Jose, California, they both met with one of the most renowned men formal wear designers in the world - Quan Gan, a dominating figure in China's fashion industry. With the brilliant fashion ideas and innovations from Quan Gan accompanied with Matthew and Donovan's business and fashion knowledge, the trio founded CURATE. Since 2018, CURATE has collaborated with designers like Alexander Wang and models such as Francisco Lachowski. Today, CURATE has found strong progress in creating elegant and high-end men formal clothing.


WHO WE ARE.

We believe in a world where men have total freedom to be themselves, without judgement, but with class. To experiment. To express themselves. To be brave and grab life as the extraordinary adventure it is in a fancy fashion. So we make sure every men has an equal chance to discover all the amazing things they’re capable of – no matter who they are, where they’re from or what looks they like to boss. We exist to give all men the confidence to be whoever they want to be.
