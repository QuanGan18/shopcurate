MY ORDER HASN'T ARRIVED, COULD YOU TELL ME WHERE IT IS?

Last updated 11/22/2019 6:11 PM

We always do our best to ensure that your orders arrive with you on time without delay. If your order hasn't arrived check out what to do next.

Step 1
Check the Delivery Date

Your estimated delivery date is in your Order Confirmation email - please allow until this date for your order to arrive. Your delivery can take place between 7am and 10pm depending on the delivery partner delivering your parcel.

Step 2
Track the Order Online

You can check the latest updates by clicking on the link in your shipping confirmation email. Alternatively, you can log into your account and view your order. Where the status shows 'Track This Order', just click the link and you'll see the latest update.

Step 3
Contact Us

If your estimated delivery date has passed, and there aren't any known delivery delays, then contact our Customer Care team the following day using the options at the bottom of this page so we can help you further.


DID WE RESOLVE YOUR ISSUE?

If we did not, please get in touch with us via Facebook, Twitter, or Email and let us know about your issue to get it resolved as soon as possible.
